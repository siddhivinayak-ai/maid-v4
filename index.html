About MaidConnect

MaidConnect was founded to bridge the gap between flat owners in need of domestic help and skilled maids looking for work. We believe in providing a transparent and efficient platform that empowers both parties to find the perfect fit. Our mission is to make the process of hiring and finding a job simple, secure, and respectful for everyone.
